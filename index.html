The Purpose: Why Do We Use It?

Used for things that are permanent, regular, or always true. Think of it as the tense for habits, routines, and facts.

1. ROUTINES & HABITS

Things you do regularly

‚úì I usually prepare lunch before heading out.
‚úì She works at a hospital on weekends.

2. PERMANENT FACTS

‚úì Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius.
‚úì The sun rises in the east.

Position in sentences:

üìå BEFORE the main verbs: I never lie.

5. PRESENT PERFECT (ÿ≠ÿßŸÑ ⁄©ÿßŸÖŸÑ)

The Purpose: Why Do We Use It?

Connects the past with the present. The timing is often unknown or the result is what matters now.

SUBJECT + HAVE/HAS + PAST PARTICIPLE (V3)

Main Uses:

1. LIFE EXPERIENCES

‚úì I have visited Paris three times.

2. RECENT ACTIONS (with present result)

‚úì I've lost my keys. (I can't get in NOW)

3. UNFINISHED TIME

‚úì I've had two meetings today. (The day isn't over)

6. PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS

The Purpose: Why Do We Use It?

Emphasizes the duration and continuity of an action.

SUBJECT + HAVE/HAS + BEEN + VERB-ING

Main Uses:

1. DURATION

‚úì I've been studying English for five years.

2. EVIDENCE of recent activity

‚úì You look tired. Have you been working out?

COMMON PREPOSITION PATTERNS

DEPEND ON
To rely on; to need
‚úì Success depends on hard work.
‚úì You can depend on me.
USE STH FOR STH
To utilize something for a purpose
‚úì What do you use the internet for?
‚úì I use my phone for studying.
PAY FOR STH
To give money for something
‚úì I paid for dinner last night.
‚úì Who's going to pay for this?
SPEND (TIME/ENERGY/MONEY) ON STH/SB
To use time, energy, or money for something/someone
‚úì I spend a lot of money on drugs.
‚úì She spends so much energy on her classes and students.
‚úì Don't spend too much time on social media.
FAMOUS FOR

‚úì Iran is famous for its carpets.
RESPONSIBLE FOR

‚úì Parents are responsible for their children.
CONCENTRATE ON
To focus attention on

To achieve a goal
